SMART HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEM

WHAT? WHY? HOW?

A smart home automation system refers to a network of connected devices and technologies that enable remote control and automation of various functions within a home. These systems use sensors, software, and communication technology to enhance convenience, energy efficiency, security, and comfort for homeowners.

Smart homes offer convenience, energy savings, and enhanced security through interconnected devices and automation, simplifying daily tasks and improving the overall quality of life for residents.

A Bluetooth-based home automation system connects appliances to a Raspberry Pi Pico using Python, allowing wireless control via smartphone. It offers portability and status feedback through a designed circuit.

In Bluetooth based home automation system the home appliances are connected to the Raspberry pi pico board at input output ports . The Bluetooth connection is established between Raspberry pi pico board and phone for wireless communication.
One circuit is designed and implemented for receiving the feedback from the phone, which indicate the status of the device. The Bluetooth connection is established between Raspberry pi pico board and phone for wireless communication.
