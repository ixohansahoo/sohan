Hey there, I'm Sohan – your friendly neighbourhood developer, part-time poet, and aspiring artist (or at least I pretend to be ).

I craft websites that behave like well-trained circus monkeys – thanks to my secret recipes of front-end devlopment , and just the right amount of caffeine.
Oh, but that's not all. I've also cracked the code to prompt engineering. Yes, Prompting AI: Where I type, it complies. It's like a comedy show, but the jokes are algorithmically sound.
Apart from that Python and I are like two peas in a pod. Its syntax is so clean that it puts my laundry skills to shame and when I'm not serenading my keyboard with Pythonic poetry, I'm crafting Java symphonies. Java's like the grand conductor of software orchestras, keeping all the instruments in harmony and now i introduce you to the secret treasure vaults of the digital universe-MySQL. With queries sharper than a sword, I wield MySQL to store, retrieve, and guard data like a pro.
And here's the kicker: amidst all the code-crunching chaos, I transform into a poet. No, really! I rhyme, I verse that might just make you smile (or groan, depending on your tolerance for wordplay).
